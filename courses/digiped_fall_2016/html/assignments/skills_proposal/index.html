Skills Development Proposal

Due: 10-20-16 by email

This will be a traditional project proposal (2 paragraphs, one on the field, one on what you will do to contribute to the field). You will pitch a topic for your skills development assignment and explain why it has potential to work well with a seminar paper. I will meet one-on-one with students (by appointment) to discuss their project ideas once I've had a chance to read these and generate feedback.

Notes for Skills Development Proposals

Things to Consider

What are the skills, competencies, methodologies/approaches/strategies, principles, habits of mind you are interested in teaching and cultivating?

Note: One way of separating a skill from a competency is that a competency would identify a threshold for performance. Example: someone who is sufficiently skilled at finding sources should be able to find X without help

How might we define literacy, fluency, and mastery for this tool or topic?

What are the critical questions you are already finding and formulating?

Which places or resources have you already identified as worth additional examination?

Some Learning Goals or Classroom Principles
  • Collaboration, sense of team camaraderie
  • Individual accountability
  • A feeling of choices
  • A feeling of ownership of course trajectory
  • Make students more aware of their own actions, choices, etc.
  • Avoid saying the same thing separately to multiple students
  • Increase grading transparency
  • Reiforce scaffolding strategies
  • Make it easier for stuggling students to catch up or get help
  • Increase engagement with some topic or way of thinking (sptial reasoning, temporal/chronological, close reading)
  • Make complex data more readable
  • Question the limits of quantification
