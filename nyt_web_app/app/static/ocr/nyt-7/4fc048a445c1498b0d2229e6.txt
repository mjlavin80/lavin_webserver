(	** The Emblem of Home," is the title of
one of the large number of short stories to appear In Scribner's August issue. It is by Eleanor Stuart, and is a tale of mystery. It has to do with the disappearance of the head of a Scottish clan and the Ions search for him. A principal character is an old family servant. The scenes are laid In Bast Africa and Arabia. IWalter Taylor has made the illustrations.
