Three new reprints of classics are announced by the Palmetto Press of Eureka Springs, Ark-, originally of Aiken, S. C. They are: Gray's “ Elegy." O'Hara's poem, " Bivouac of the Dead,” and “ The Blessed Damozel.” The booklets have been printed on Whatman imported handmade paper from new fonts of type. Each contains a frontispiece and illuminated title page. Mrs. J. S. Couper has also designed the initial for Gray's “ Elegy."
