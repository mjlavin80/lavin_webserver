A S Barnes & Co. announce for publication April 1 a small history of American life by George Cary Eggleston, entitled *' Our First Century-” Among the subjects to which the author gives special attention are what the colonists at Jamestown and Plymouth wore and ate, what their education and modes of thought were, how they worked and how they amused themselves. Emphasis is placed on the life of the times rather than upon politics or war. The book will be fully illustrated.
