The Harpers announce that their limited ' edition of “ The Poems and Ballads of Algernon Charles Swinburne/’ of which 149 sets of six volumes each were published during the last week in December, has been exhausted, and that sundry sets have already been resold at a premium by the original purchasers- The sets were bound in gray boards and vellum, and printed upon fine white paper, with a rubricked title page and uncut edges. The letter press included Mr. Swinburne's introductory epistle on the art of poetry and all the other matter of the regular Harper edition.
