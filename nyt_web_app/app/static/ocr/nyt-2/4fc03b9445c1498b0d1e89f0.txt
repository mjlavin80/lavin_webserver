“ A Military and Naval Dictionary," by Major John P. Wisser of the United States Army and H. C. Gauss of the Navy Department, Is now being sent out by the L. R. Hamersly Company. It contains authentic and plainly worded definitions of all the terms used to-day in the military and naval services, and brief definitions of the powers appertaining to each department of the United States Government, and the duties of all the Government officials.
