Prof. Charles Bigg has put into book form, with a preface, notes, and an excursus. his four lectures on “ The Church’s Task Under the Roman Empire.” He sketches broadly the nature of the task before the Church when she began to evangelize the Graeco-Roman world. The writer deals principally with the questions. What the Roman Empire was, and what it made of Its subjects; What each individual Christian was, down to the moment of his conversion and afterward. Henry Frowde is publishing the volume at the Clarendon Press.
