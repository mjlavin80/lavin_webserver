Mrs. Anne Botsford Comstock, author of '* How to Keep Bees." which is published by Doubleday, Page & Co., Is one of the best-known nature-study authorities in this country. She is Assistant Professor of extension work in Nature Study at Cornell University, and lectures each year at Lcland Stanford University* Mrs. Comstock is perhaps the best-known female wood engraver of insects, having exhibited at Paris, Chicago, and Buffalo; at the last place she received a bronze medal. Her husband, John Henry Comstock, holds the professorship of Entomology at Cornell.
