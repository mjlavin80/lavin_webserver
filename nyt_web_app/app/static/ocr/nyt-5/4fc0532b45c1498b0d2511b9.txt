Mr. John Lane of the Bodley Head sailed for Liverpool on the Baltic this week, after an unusually long absence from his London office in Vigo Street. During his stay in New York he incorporated the American branch of his English publishing business under the stylo of the John Lane Company. Mr. Rutger Bleecker Jewett is managing Director, while Mr. Robert W. De Forest and Mr. Acosta Nichols of the banking house of Spencer Trask & Co. are Directors.
