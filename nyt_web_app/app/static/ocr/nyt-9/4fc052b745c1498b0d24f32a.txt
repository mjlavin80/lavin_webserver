About the middle of September the Harpers will publish two additional volumes of “ The American Nation ” edited by Albert Bushneli Hart* According to the publishers the series has been adopted for use in many colleges; hence the haste in getting as many volumes as possible ready at this time, when the institutions of learning are about opening their doors. The issues of this month are Volume IX.,
“ The American .Revolution,” by Prof. Claude Halstead Van Tyne of the University of Michigan, and Volume X., “ The Confederation and the Constitution,” by Andrew C. McLaughlin, Director of the Bureau of Historical Research, Carnegie Institution, Washington, D. C. The titles indicate sufficiently the scope of the volumes.
