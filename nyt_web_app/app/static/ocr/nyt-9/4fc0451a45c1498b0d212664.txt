Justus Miles Forman, author of *' The Garden of L.k*s " and “ Tommy Carteret,’* has a new story called ** Tho Island of Enchantment.** The romance belongs not to New York and Paris, but to the fourteenth century and the Islands of the Adriatic. The hero is a young officer sent by the Dogre of Venice against a beautiful and wicked lady. The story is full of passionate doings nnd conflicts of love and honor such as Mr. Forman loves. The book, which is illustrated by Howard Pyle, will be published by the Harpers Sept. 21.
