The final volume of the Biographical Edition of Robert Louis Stevenson’s works* edited by Mrs. Stevenson, will contain 4* Essays of Travel and in the Art of Writing." There will be twenty essays In all, fourteen of travel and the remaining half dozen of writing. Several of . them have never before been published In a trade edition. The middle of the month will see this and the other three last volumes In the edition come from Charles Scribner’s. Sons.
