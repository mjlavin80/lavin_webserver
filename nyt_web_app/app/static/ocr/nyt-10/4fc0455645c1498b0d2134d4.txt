The Macmillan Company is publishing to-day " Civil War and Reconstruction in Alabama,” by Prof. Walter L. Fleming of the West Virginia University. This Is a study of the political, social, and economic effects of the rebellion and reconstruction upon institutions that existed *' befo* the war.” The volume, which is one of the Columbia University Press publications, is illustrated with eighteen maps, forty half-tones, fac similes of documents, &c.
