This is the third volume of Prof. Liberty H. Bailey's series of popular botanical texts, the first two of which, by Prof. Bailey, dealt with '* Lessons with Plants " and " Botany.” '* Experiments with Plants ” is the work of W. J. V. Osterhout, Assistant Professor of Botany in the University-of California. It has been planned to suggest and explain simple ways by which the student could be set at the working out of real problems In the growth and behavior of plants. Prof. Osterhout has chosen “ familiar ” plants and utensils, and in his introduction gives an outline of work in the keeping- of note3 on experiments. The book is illustrated from photographs, drawings, diagrams, &c., 253 pictures in all, and there is a very full index. It bears the Imprint of The Macmillan Company.
